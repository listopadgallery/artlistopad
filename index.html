Estate Framing
The archive comprises approximately 300 paintings. Not all works are published publicly; the estate follows a controlled release model intended to preserve value and curatorial coherence.

The site is designed not as a marketplace, but as a collector-facing presentation layer. A concise public selection introduces the work; a broader inventory remains available by request.

This structure makes future growth simple: new images can be uploaded to GitHub, then linked through a single data file. The gallery pages and individual artwork pages update automatically from that record.
