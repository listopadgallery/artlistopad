Available works from the estate. Prices available upon request. Works by Alexey Listopad are held in museum and private collections in Russia, Lithuania, Poland, France, Germany, the United States, England, China, and Australia.

Exhibitions
The work of Alexey Listopad has been presented in institutional and gallery contexts across Moscow, St. Petersburg, and Europe. Selected exhibitions include: — Dolgoprudny Historical and Art Museum · “Eternal Themes” (2020) — Dolgoprudny Historical and Art Museum · “Under the Blue Sky” (2017) — Central House of Artists, Moscow · Personal Exhibition (2019) — Union of Artists of Russia · Personal Exhibition (2015) — Nida, Lithuania · Personal Exhibition (2010) — Central House of Artists, Moscow · Personal Exhibition (2009) — Dolgoprudny Historical and Art Museum · Personal Exhibition (2007)
Estate
The archive comprises approximately 300 paintings. Not all works are published publicly; the estate follows a measured, selective release strategy.

This website is intended as a collector-facing presentation layer rather than a marketplace. A concise public selection introduces the work. A broader inventory remains available by request.

That structure protects value, preserves curatorial coherence, and allows future releases to be staged with greater precision.
